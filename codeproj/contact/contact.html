How to make an Ice Cream

*2 cups whole milk

*1 cup sugar

*1/4 cup fat-free powdered milk

*8 eggs (yolks only needed)

*1 cup heavy whipping cream (OR half-and-half - regular or fat-free OR light cream for lighter

more ice cream, more like gelato)

*1 teaspoon vanilla extract

*3 cups of prepared fruit (strawberries, peaches, raspberries, mangoes, or whatever you have! See step 7 for details.
